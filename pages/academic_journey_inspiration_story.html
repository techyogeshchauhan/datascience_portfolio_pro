Challenges Overcome & Lessons Learned

Every challenge was a stepping stone to growth and wisdom

Major Challenges

Financial Constraints

Coming from a middle-class family, funding my education while supporting family needs required careful planning and multiple scholarships.

Balancing Research & Teaching

Managing PhD research deadlines while maintaining teaching quality required developing exceptional time management and prioritization skills.

Imposter Syndrome

Transitioning from student to researcher and teacher simultaneously brought moments of self-doubt that required building confidence through small wins.

Key Lessons

Persistence Over Perfection

Progress matters more than perfection. Every small step forward, even with mistakes, builds toward the larger goal.

Community & Mentorship

Building relationships with peers, mentors, and students creates a support network that accelerates learning and provides guidance during difficult times.

Embrace the Unknown

Research means venturing into uncharted territory. Embracing uncertainty and being comfortable with not knowing everything is essential for discovery.
